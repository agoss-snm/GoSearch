What is the game of go?

Go is an abstract strategy board game for two players in which the aim is to surround more territory than the opponent.

The game was invented in China more than 2,500 years ago and is believed to be the oldest board game continuously played to the present day.

Why should I learn to play Go?

  • Go teaches mental skills such as patience, discipline, and open-mindedness.
  • This has been found to be helpful in avoiding degenerative brain diseases such as Alzheimers.
  • Create opportunities for creating and strengthening friendships and also strengthening the mind.
